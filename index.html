Mental Health Tips

Managing Anxiety

Practice deep breathing and mindfulness to stay grounded.

Coping with Stress

Take breaks, exercise, and talk to someone you trust.

Better Sleep

Create a calming bedtime routine and avoid screens before bed.

Connect Socially

Schedule time with friends or join community groups to build support.

Practice Gratitude

Write down three things you’re grateful for each day to boost positivity.

Stay Active

Incorporate a short walk, yoga, or stretching to elevate your mood.
